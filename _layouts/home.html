Why Kuno?

Easy to Learn

A simple framework built on industry standards makes way for a wealth of training resources from known experts. Lots of example documents from onboarding to deployment.

Portable

Once built, easily use the same code and host with ASP.NET Core, Nancy FX, Akka.NET, Azure Functions, etc. Plug in different databases such as SQL and MongoDB. When the newest technology comes out, you'll be ready for it.

As your product received more adoption, have confidence in the ability to scale features and services. Scaling requires great monitoring and metrics. All provided with kuno.
